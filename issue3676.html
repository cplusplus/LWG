Discussion:

In N4901 28.3.1.1 [locale.general] paragraph 8:

A locale constructed from a name string (such as "POSIX"), or from parts of two named locales, has a name; all others do not.

In 28.3.1.4 [locale.members], for 

template<class Facet> locale combine(const locale& other) const;

it says:

Remarks: The resulting locale has no name.

It seems the combine() interface does not match the general design description.
