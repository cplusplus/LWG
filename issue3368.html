3368. LWG 3264 and opting-out of sized_range

Section: 24.3.9 [range.prim.size] Status: New Submitter: Johel Ernesto Guerrero Peña Opened: 2020-01-07 Last modified: 2020-01-14

Priority: Not Prioritized

View all issues with New status.

Discussion:

LWG 3264 removes the explicit disable_sized_range check in safe_range because it is subsumed by its explicit validity check of ranges::size. However, when ranges::size's check of disable_sized_range is false, it evaluates to make-unsigned-like(ranges::end(E) - ranges::begin(E)) given some other conditions. The net result it not being able to opt-out of sized_range.

Proposed resolution:

This wording is relative to N4842.
